# Project-Management-Compliance-Coursework2-Report

## 🔍 Overview
This project presents the design and development of a **Smart Home Estate** integrating IoT-based technologies, automation, and cybersecurity principles. It was developed as part of a university coursework focusing on **project management, compliance, and secure system design**.

The estate includes multiple housing types, each tailored to different user needs, with a strong emphasis on **energy efficiency, safety, and intelligent automation**.

---

##  Estate Composition
The smart estate consists of:

- **6 × 4-Bedroom Family Homes**  
  Designed for comfort, security, and advanced automation.

- **8 × 2-Bedroom Starter Homes**  
  Cost-effective smart homes for first-time buyers and small families.

- **Warden-Assisted Elderly Home**  
  A specialized **Ambient Assisted Living (AAL)** environment supporting elderly residents through health monitoring and assistive technologies.

---

##  Key Features

###  Smart Automation
- Smart lighting and climate control  
- Voice control integration (Amazon Alexa)  
- Centralized mobile app control  

###  Security Systems
- AI-powered CCTV with motion detection  
- Smart locks and access control  
- Real-time monitoring systems  

###  Elderly Care Technologies
- Smart mattresses (fall detection & sleep monitoring)  
- AI fall detection cameras  
- Smart medication system  
- Centralized **Warden Monitoring Dashboard**  

###  Energy & Sustainability
- Solar panel integration  
- AI-based energy optimization  
- Reduced environmental impact  

---

##  System Integration
All devices are connected through a **central IoT platform**, enabling:
- Real-time monitoring  
- Automation and remote control  
- Data-driven decision-making  

---

##  Cost Analysis & ROI
Although the project involves a **high initial investment**, particularly for elderly care technologies, it offers strong long-term returns:

- Reduced energy costs (solar systems)  
- Lower healthcare and emergency costs  
- Improved operational efficiency  
- Scalable and sustainable infrastructure

##  Cybersecurity & Compliance
This project considers key cybersecurity principles, including:
- Secure IoT device integration  
- Data protection and privacy  
- Risk management strategies  
- Alignment with standards such as **ISO/IEC 27001**
